Why you need a website

The importance of a well designed website can make the difference if your business will MAKE or BREAK it.

People search the internet for businesses, productsm services, etc. The days of relying on phone books are over. Looking good is important because your website is often your first impression you give potentioal customers.

1

Planning

Firstly, I will start of by gather information to establish the websites purpose, main goals and target audience. With this information I begin to sketch out a wireframe in Adobe XD. This is a blueprint of the content that will be appearing on the website. I will copywrite text assets.

2

Design

I will now move on to establish a color palette, finding fitting typography, review and decide what images should be used. The final web design will be created as a prototype in Adobe XD. I will then contact the customer to get the design approved so we can continue to the final stage.

3

Development

This is were the magic happens. With the power of coding I will take the agreed upon design and create a live website. The website will be coded for mobile first as this is the most important device to have a working website on. Then I will continue until all devices from mobile to desktop are covered.
